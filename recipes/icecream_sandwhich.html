Ice Cream Sandwich

A tasty chocolate treat to beat the summer heat!

This chocolate treat is bound to make your tastebuds sing! The cookie is made of decadent chocolate reminiscent of childhood memories. The inside is filled with a thick layer of classic vanilla ice cream ready to be enjoyed on a hot summer evening!

Ingredients

  • 1/3 cup sifted Dutch-process cocoa powder
  • 3 tablespoons unsalted butter, softened
  • 1/4 cup white sugar
  • 2 tablespoons light brown sugar
  • 1/2 teaspoon pure vanilla extract
  • 1/3 cup white milk, at room temperature
  • 6 scoops vanilla ice cream

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C). Line the baking sheet with a silicone liner. Spray liner with cooking spray and rub it in to be sure it's completely covered.
  2. Whisk 1/2 cup plus 1 tablespoon flour, salt, baking soda, and cocoa together in a bowl until thoroughly combined. Sift mixture if it seems clumpy or chunky.
  3. Mix butter, both sugars, and vanilla in another bowl with a spatula until you have a smooth paste. Add flour mixture along with milk and stir until batter is smooth and stiff. Clean off the spatula with a cookie scoop and drop 12 scoops of batter onto the prepared baking sheet.
  4. Transfer baking sheet into the freezer until cookies are firm, about 15 minutes.
  5. Remove from the freezer and remove cookies from the pan by lifting th silicone liner carefully under eahc one. Poke 10 to 12 holes in the surface of each cookie with the sharp edge of a chopstick. return to the freezer until ready to assemble.
  6. When ready to assemble, remove from the freezer. Turn one cookie upside down, place 1 scoop ice cream on top, and sandwich with a second cookie. Serve immediately or wrap in plastic wrap and freeze for 3 hours before serving. Repeat to make remaining ice cream sandwiches.
  7. Enjoy!
