Strawberry Cupcakes

A delicous set of strawberry cupcakes sure to fill your summer with happiness!

This is a strawberry cupcake recipes sure to bring joy to your summer afternoons. Each cupcake is made up of soft fluffy cake baked to perfection. Each golden brown cupcake is topped with a sweet tower of handmade stawberry buttercream frosting. The finishing touch to such a magnificent dessert creatoin: a sweet plump strawberry!

Ingredients

Cupcakes
  • 1 1/4 ounces of freeze-dried strawberries
  • 3/4 cup all-purpose flour
  • 1 1/2 teaspoons baking powder
  • 1/4 teaspoon baking soda
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 cup unsalted butter, room temperature
  • 1 1/3 cups white sugar
  • 3 eggs, room temperature
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 2/3 cup whole milk, room temperature
Frosting
  • 1 cup fresh strawberries
  • 1 cup butter
  • 1 cup confectioners sigar,sifted
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 2 1/2 cups confectioners sugar, sifted,divided

Steps

Cupcakes
  1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C). Line muffin tins with 20 cupcake liners (for mini cupcakes, line 72 mini muffin tins).
  2. Grind strawberries into a fine powder using a coffee grinder or food processor. Whisk strawberry powder. all-purpose flour, cake flour, baking powder, baking soda, and salt together in a bowl.
  3. Beat butter and sugar in a bowl with an electric mixer until light and fluffy. Mixture should be noticeably lighter in color. Add room-temperature eggs ine at a time, allowing each egg to blend into butter mixture before adding the next. Beat in vanilla extract.
  4. Stir flour mixture, alternately with the milk, inot butter mixture until just incorporated. Fill lined tins with cupcake batter.
  5. Bake in preheated oven until cupcakes spring back when gently touched with a fingertip or toothpick inserted in the center comes out clean, 16 to 18 minutes for regular cupcakes, 8 to 10 minutes for mini cupcakes.
Frosting
  1. Place strawberries in a blender; puree until smooth.
  2. Transfer strawberry puree to a saucepan over medium heat; bring to a boil, stirring often, until ouree is reduced by at least half, about 20 minuntes. Remove from heat and cool completely
  3. Beat butter with an electric mixer in a bowl until light and fluffy.
  4. Beat 1 cup of confectioners sugar into butter until just blended.
  5. Beat 2 tablespoons strawberry puree and vanilla extract into butter mixture until just blended.
  6. Repeat with 1 cup confectioners sugar, followed by 2 tablespoons strawberry puree two more times.
  7. Beat last 1/2 cup confectionerssugar into mixture until just blended.
  8. Use frosting to decorate cupcake in desired manner.
