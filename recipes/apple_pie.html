Apple Pie

A classic that never fails!

The classic apple pie is a deserrt sure to never disappoint at any gathering! The inside is filled with layer upon layer of sweet crisp apple filling. The filling is complimented with a golden flakey crust, bringing this dynamic duo inot existence. All you need now is a cold scoop of vanilla ince cream!

Ingredients

  • 1 (14.3 ounce) package pastry for 9-inch double crust pie
  • 3/4 cup white sugar, or more to taste
  • 1 teaspoon ground cinnamon
  • 6 cups thinly sliced apples
  • 1 tablespoon butter
