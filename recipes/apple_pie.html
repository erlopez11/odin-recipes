Apple Pie

A classic that never fails!

The classic apple pie is a deserrt sure to never disappoint at any gathering! The inside is filled with layer upon layer of sweet crisp apple filling. The filling is complimented with a golden flakey crust, bringing this dynamic duo inot existence. All you need now is a cold scoop of vanilla ince cream!

Ingredients

  • 1 (14.3 ounce) package pastry for 9-inch double crust pie
  • 3/4 cup white sugar, or more to taste
  • 1 teaspoon ground cinnamon
  • 6 cups thinly sliced apples
  • 1 tablespoon butter

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 450 degrees F (230 degrees C).
  2. Line a 9-inche pie dish with one pastry crust; set second one aside.
  3. Combine 3/4 cup sugar and cinnamon in a small bowl. Add more sugar if your apples are tart.
  4. Layer apple slices in the prepared pie dish, sprinkling each layer with cinnamon-sugar mixture. Dot top layer with small pieces of butter. Cover with top crust.
  5. Bake pie on lowest rack of preheated oven for 10 minutes. Redduce oven temperature to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C) and continue baking until golden brown and filling bubbles, 30 to 35 minutes more.
  6. Serve warm or cold.
